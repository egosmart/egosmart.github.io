How we help?

Idea screening

We at eGO SMART come across plentiful ideas everyday from future entrepreneurs. We believe in the potential of them. Our team evaluates each idea against some specific criteria to diagnose their achievability. eGo SMART provides excellent mentoring, constant assistance, means to reach right investors, and cutting edge technology required to translate the ideas into an actual product.

Structure

To shape up the idea for further development we either create a structure of MVP or prototype. Prototype is an initial product which gets tested in various hypothetical market scenarios to ascertain the feasibility of the idea. Minimum viable product is the simplest form of your idea that can be marketed as a product. It is an early version of the product in the idea development process so that any changes suggested by the target audience can be implemented. We create MVPs for product, website, softwares, and mobile applications.

Model validation

We build a business model for the prototype or minimum viable product to be tested against various market constraints.We pass the model through a series of tests using our model validation tools. We create a plan for the market acceptance of the product, generate authentic reviews of the target markets, conduct competitive analysis, research existing demand of the product, initiate crowd funding campaigns to get an idea about the readiness of the target market.

Expansion

To spread the word of mouth our marketing department creates marketing strategy for the right product placement at the right time to the right audience. We create marketing plans out beyond the limitations of physical market for a better market share. Our network is based on social media marketing which has a stronger impact. For infused capital requirements we bring in the investors, help you assemble the right talent on board, maintain a consistent brand image of your product, build a referral network and cultivate ads which appeal to emotions.

Marketing

Our marketing experts are well equipped professionals who will help you expand your customer reach & flaunt your ideas to the world.

Get afloat

A successful product launch requires the coordinated efforts of many teams and departments across the company—not only product management and development, but also marketing, sales, customer support and finance. It takes planned and coordinated efforts to debut a new product to the market and make that product generally available for purchase. We assist you in the process of product launch and gather valuable feedback from early users, create momentum and industry recognition for the company. We plan your product launch strategically and in advance. Every organization’s circumstances are unique and so we design unique product launch designs suited for each company.
